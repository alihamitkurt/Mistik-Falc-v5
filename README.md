# Mistik-Falc-v5
Our project is called the Mystic Fortune Teller. People can easily come to us for spiritual and physical support in their daily lives or during times of trauma. We do this with our dedicated fortune teller team. Of course, we charge you a small amount in return, but don't worry; our site has mini games that you can use to earn this small amount and motivate yourself. Also, if you're curious about how your love life or business life will go, you can find those on our site. To run the site, download the zip file, create a folder in Visual Studio, and type the following code into the terminal: "npm run dev". After that, the site will be active. We extend our thanks to the Mystic Fortune Teller team.
